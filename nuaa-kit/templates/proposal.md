## 1. Background & Need

### About NUAA

NSW Users & AIDS Association (NUAA) is a peer-led organization with over 30 years of experience working alongside people who use drugs. We are the state-wide peak body representing the interests and rights of people who use illicit drugs. NUAA operates on harm reduction principles, prioritizing the health, dignity, and rights of our community.

**Our Approach**:

- **Peer-led**: Programs designed and delivered by people with lived experience
- **Harm reduction**: Pragmatic, evidence-based approaches that meet people where they're at
- **Advocacy**: Challenging stigma, discrimination, and unjust policies
- **Community**: Building connection, support, and collective voice
