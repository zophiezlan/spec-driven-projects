## 7. Impact Measurement Framework

### What We'll Measure

**Process Indicators** (Are we doing what we planned?):

- Number of sessions delivered
- Attendance rates
- Participant satisfaction
- Staff/peer feedback

**Output Indicators** (What did we deliver?):

- Participants engaged
- Resources distributed
- Referrals made
- Partnerships activated

**Outcome Indicators** (What changed for participants?):

- Knowledge gained (pre/post surveys)
- Skills developed (self-assessment)
- Wellbeing improved (validated scales)
- Connections strengthened (network mapping)

**Impact Indicators** (Broader changes):

- Community stigma reduced (community surveys)
- Service accessibility improved (system data)
- Policy influence (advocacy outcomes)
